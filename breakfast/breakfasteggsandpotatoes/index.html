BreakFast Eggs and Potatoes

Looking for a satisfying breakfast? Eggs and potatoes are the perfect vegetarian-friendly choice to fuel your morning and keep you full longer.

This simple recipe takes just 20 minutes to prepare and uses ingredients you likely already have at home.

Ingredients

  • 2 Potatoes
  • 2 Eggs
  • Cooking oil
  • Season with your favorite spices! I typically use salt, pepper, garlic powder, paprika, and sazon.
  • Cheese of your choice! I typically use Mozzarella Cheese

Direction

  1. Start by washing the two potatoes, then cut them into small cubes.
  2. Next, grab a pan, place it on the stove, and drizzle in your preferred type of oil.
  3. Turn the stove to high heat.
  4. Add the potatoes to the pan and season them with all your preferred seasonings.
  5. Mix the potatoes well to ensure they are evenly coated with the seasonings and oil.
  6. Cover the pan and reduce the stove heat to medium.

    7. Important: Keep checking the potatoes, stirring occasionally, and add more oil if needed!

  7. When the potatoes are about 85% cooked, grab a bowl and crack the two eggs into it.
  8. Season the eggs and whisk them together in the bowl.
  9. Pour the eggs into the pan and stir them together with the potatoes.
  10. Reduce the stove heat to low, then sprinkle your cheese over the eggs and potatoes. Cover the pan and let it cook for about 2 minutes.
  11. Turn off the heat, grab your plate, and transfer the eggs and potatoes onto it. Enjoy!

I hope this breakfast leaves you feeling satisfied. Enjoy!
